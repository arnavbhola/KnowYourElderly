Know Your Elderly

Community Driven Social Service

Our Mission

Recently, we have entered a global pandemic that is affecting hundreds of millions of lives across the planet. The most effected group of people through these troubling times, are the elderly. We wanted to make sure that these people get the help they need, while staying safe at home. Some seniors are not capable of taking care of themselves and have a hard time performing daily task such as getting grocery's. Our website helps gives these elderly people the support they need through these times. This service is based solely off of volunteers who would like to ensure the safety of those who are endangered by this crisis the most by providing them with day day to day necessities.

Community

Our community is incredibly important to us. We want to help out our neighbors in their time of need, because one day they may pay that favor back to us, or pass it onwards. Since we care so much about our own communities, we also want to eventually reach other communities around the world that need help just as much, if not more than we do..

Frequently Asked Questions

How does this service accommodate users to our current global pandemic?

This service ensures that the volunteers will have minimal contact while helping the elderly and making sure remind volunteers to stay safe.

Who is the audience of this site?

This site is targeted toward people who are incapable for caring for themselves on a daily bases and thoes kind-hearted inviduals who would like to help out and give back to the community.

Is there any payment involved in this program?

No, this service is based solely on the kind-hearted volunteers who take the time out of their day to help the elderly with activities they struggle with.

Is this project an international project?

As of now, this project is a local project focused on helping those in out community. We hope to expand in the near future to maybe test international waters.
